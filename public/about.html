About wayexam.com

Last updated · 2026

wayexam.com is a free practice hub for four of the most widely recognized professional certification exams in South Korea: PMP (Project Management Professional), CPLA (Certified Public Labor Attorney, 공인노무사), Industrial Safety Consultant (산업안전지도사), and Industrial Health Consultant (산업보건지도사).

What we do

Each of the four exam tracks runs as its own dedicated practice site. You get hundreds of authentic past-exam questions organized by year and subject, automatic accuracy statistics that show you which topics need more work, an auto-built wrong-answer notebook, and a one-click bookmark system you can review later. The PMP track adds five-language switching (English, Korean, Chinese, Spanish, Japanese) for international candidates preparing for the same exam in different markets.

Why this exists

Korean professional exams have, historically, been a market dominated by paid offline academies. Past questions exist but are scattered across PDF files, photocopied booklets, and forum threads. We pulled them into a single web platform where you can practice the same way you take the real exam — single questions, immediate scoring, and review built into the workflow rather than bolted on at the end.

Who runs it

wayexam.com is operated by an independent team based in Korea. We are not affiliated with the Project Management Institute, KOSHA, the Korean Bar Association, or any other licensing body. All exam content is reproduced for study purposes from publicly published past papers and is attributed where required.

How we keep the lights on

The core practice experience is free on every track. We display Google AdSense advertising on some pages and offer an optional Premium tier (extended analytics, no ads on quiz pages) on the exam sites where it is available. We do not sell email addresses, do not run pop-ups, and do not require credit-card details for anonymous trial use.

Contact

For questions about a specific past-exam item, the Report button on each question is the fastest path — it pings the moderator directly. For partnerships, content licensing, or anything else, email songdoinfo@naver.com or use our contact page.
