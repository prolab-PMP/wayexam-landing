Contact wayexam.com

Last updated · 2026

Email

The fastest way to reach the team is by email:

songdoinfo@naver.com

We try to respond within two business days. Email tends to be the right channel for partnership inquiries, content licensing questions, press requests, and bug reports that affect more than one user.

Report a specific question issue

If you spot a typo, a wrong answer, a translation problem, or a question that no longer matches the current exam scope, the fastest fix is the Report button that appears on every question card inside each of the four exam sites. Those reports route directly to the moderation queue and get triaged together with other reports on the same question, which is usually faster than email.

Account or billing questions

If you have an account on one of the exam sites (PMP, CPLA, Safety, Health), please mention which site and the email address you signed up with when you write — that lets us find your record without a back-and-forth. For Premium subscriptions, refunds, or validity-period extensions, the operator email above is the right channel.

Press & partnerships

Email with a brief description of the request. We are not currently running paid sponsorships on quiz pages, but cross-promotion partnerships with adjacent ed-tech or career-services brands are open to discussion.

Mailing address

For physical mail, please request the postal address by email first so we can route it correctly.
