Exam Guides

In-depth overviews of the four Korean professional exams covered on wayexam.com — eligibility, subjects, pass rates, and how to prepare.

PMP — Project Management Professional

The international standard for project managers, administered by PMI. Covers PMBOK 7 principles and the 2026 ECO transition to PMBOK 8. Multilingual practice available.

CPLA — 공인노무사 (Certified Public Labor Attorney)

Korea's licensed labor-law advisor credential. Two-stage exam covering labor law, social insurance, HR management, and a written essay defense.

SAFETY — 산업안전지도사 (Industrial Safety Consultant)

Senior-level workplace-safety credential under Korea's Occupational Safety and Health Act. Covers machine, electrical, construction, and chemical safety, plus risk management.

HEALTH — 산업보건지도사 (Industrial Health Consultant)

Occupational-health counterpart to the Safety Consultant. Covers industrial hygiene, ergonomics, occupational disease, and health-management systems.
