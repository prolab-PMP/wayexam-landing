Privacy Policy

Effective date · 2026

What this policy covers

This policy explains what information wayexam.com collects when you visit the hub site (this domain) and how that information is used. Each of the four exam sites linked from the hub (PMP, CPLA, Industrial Safety, Industrial Health) maintains its own separate privacy policy that applies once you visit that site.

What we collect on this hub

The hub site is mostly static. We collect:

  • Standard server logs: the requested URL, your IP address, the referring page, your browser user-agent, and the request timestamp. These are retained for up to 30 days for security and abuse-prevention purposes.
  • Cookies set by third-party advertising (Google AdSense): see the section below.

The hub does not require an account, does not run any analytics SDK, and does not record what links you click after leaving for an exam site.

Google AdSense

This site displays advertising served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to personalize ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time at google.com/settings/ads or via aboutads.info. We do not have access to any personal information Google may collect through advertising cookies.

Children

The site is not directed at children under 14. If you believe a child has provided personal information through wayexam.com, please contact us at songdoinfo@naver.com and we will delete it.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you by emailing the address above. We respond to verified requests within 30 days.

Changes

Material changes to this policy will be reflected in the "Effective date" above and announced on the home page for at least seven days before taking effect.
